## Booking Steps

<Steps>
  <Step title="Sign in to your account">
    Go to https://prathamraval.com and log in.
  </Step>
  <Step title="Select Home Care">
    Choose 'Book Home Care Visit' from the dashboard.
  </Step>
  <Step title="Choose date and time">
    Pick your preferred slot from the calendar.
  </Step>
  <Step title="Confirm details">
    Enter any required service details and click 'Confirm Booking'.
  </Step>
  <Step title="View booking">
    Your appointment will appear in your dashboard immediately.
  </Step>
</Steps>
