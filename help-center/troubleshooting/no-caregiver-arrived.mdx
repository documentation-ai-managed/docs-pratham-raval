---
title: No Caregiver Arrived
description: What to do if your PRATHAM RAVAL caregiver is late or missed.
---

## Symptoms

- Caregiver did not show up
- No notification received

## Quick Fix

<Steps>
  <Step title="Check your booking details">
    Log in and confirm your scheduled date/time in your dashboard.
  </Step>
  <Step title="Contact support">
    Email support@prathamraval.com or call helpline (+91-12345-67890) for instant help.
  </Step>
  <Step title="Reschedule">
    Use the app or website to pick a new available slot.
  </Step>
</Steps>
